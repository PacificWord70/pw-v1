Projects

Movie Love

Movie Love is a simple webpage I created that a user can use to create a list of their favorite movies. The user can enter the movie title and year. The list comes with some button features: Star, Remove, and IMDB link.

Upgrade Indy is a project my team and I created during Indy Civil Hacks 2017. This site shows how businesses can elevate the community around them. Indy is a growing city and gaining many new companies and this can really help the citizens. We gathered data from the OpenIndy data portal about land zoning and crime. We allow businesses to view where similar companies to their's are located. I worked in the visualizatitons of the data comprized of the graph and dynamic map.
